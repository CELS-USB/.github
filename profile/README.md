# CELS – Center of Explainable Learning Systems

CELS is a research laboratory at the Faculty of Science, University of South Bohemia in České Budějovice, Czech Republic.

The laboratory focuses on machine learning, neural modelling, explainable artificial intelligence, dynamical systems, industrial diagnostics, biomedical signal processing, and educational activities in artificial intelligence and data science.

## Research Areas

* Explainable Machine Learning
* Learning Entropy
* Linear and Higher Order Neural Units (LNU/HONU)
* Neural Stability and Convergence
* Dynamical Systems Modelling and Identification
* Physics-Informed and Interpretable AI
* Industrial Monitoring and Diagnostics
* Biomedical Signal Processing
* Adaptive Control and Reinforcement Learning

## Open-Source Software

Current software projects include:

* IPMAI Cycle Monitor
* Learning Entropy Tools
* HONU Educational Examples
* Research and Teaching Software
* Experimental Explainable AI Frameworks

## Research Activities

CELS develops methods and software for interpretable modelling, monitoring, prediction, anomaly detection, and analysis of complex technical and biomedical systems. Particular emphasis is placed on explainability, stability, physical consistency, and practical deployment in real-world applications.
